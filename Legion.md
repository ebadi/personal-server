**Hybrid graphics issue**

There are two graphics cards on the PC:

- Intel Corporation Raptor Lake-S UHD Graphics
- NVIDIA® GeForce RTX™ 5060 Ti GPU

---

### Issue 1: Secure Boot

Press **F12* to enter the BIOS → **Security**:

- Secure Boot: Disabled

I also performed these steps (which may not be necessary):

- Secure Rollback: No
- Windows UEFI Firmware Update: Disabled
- Reset Platform to Setup Mode

---

### Issue 2: Daisy chaining over DisplayPort

Daisy chaining over DisplayPort works only on Windows, **not*- on Linux.

On Linux, after installing the OS, the screen turns black. You can press **Ctrl + Alt + Backspace*- to return to the login page, but no driver is running, which is inconvenient.

dmesg throw nvidia related errors.

---

### Solution

Do not use daisy chaining. Instead, use two separate cables from the PC:

- HDMI from the NVIDIA GPU (not DisplayPort)
- DisplayPort from the Intel GPU
